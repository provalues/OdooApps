Material Requisition Management


This module enhances material management in Odoo by streamlining the process of requesting, approving, and tracking material requisitions. It allows departments to submit requests for materials, which then undergo a structured approval workflow.
Key Features:
- Multi-Level Approval Workflow: Requisitions move through defined stages, allowing department and procurement managers to approve or reject requests with ease.
- Role-Based Access Control: Ensures only authorized users can submit, approve, or reject material requests.
